To Study and measure the Voltage, Current, Power and Power factor in parallel RLC circuit

Instructions

  • Step 1: Make connections as per the instructions given below.

    • 1-23, 2-24
    • 3-25, 4-26
    • 5-25, 6-9, 6-10, 7-18
    • 7-18, 8-11
    • 11-13, 13-15, 12-17
    • 18-20, 14-19, 16-21
    • 20-22, 26-7

  • Note: Click on the label to delete connection for the corresponding node.

  • Step 2: Now, Check the connections by clicking on ‘CHECK’ button.

    • If the connections are ‘Invalid connections’ click on corresponding node to remove the connection.

    • And if the connections are ‘Right Connections’ then follow the below steps.

  • Step 3: Turn on the MCB.

  • Step 4:Switch on the variac by clicking on the button.

  • Step 5:Click on the variac knob to set the voltage at 220V.

  • Step 6:Now, Click on ‘ADD’ button to add the readings in the observation table.

  • Step 7:In Calculations section, We have to manually calculate the values by using the formula and verify it with the help of Verify button.

FORMULAS

\[R = \frac{V}{I_R} \]

\[X_L = \frac{V}{I_L}\]

\[X_C = \frac{V}{I_C}\]

\[ L = \frac{X_L}{2\pi{f}}\]

\[C = \frac {1}{2\pi{fX_C}}\]

\[Z = \frac{1}{\sqrt {\left(\frac{1}{R}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{1}{X_L} - \frac{1}{X_C}\right)^2}}\]

\[COS\phi = \frac{Z}{R}\]

\[S = VI\]

\[Q= VISin\phi\]

\[R = 50 \Omega \]

\[X_L = 6.28\Omega\]

\[X_C = 636.94 \Omega\]

\[ L = 20 mH \]

\[C = 5\mu{F} \]

\[Z = 6.41 \Omega \]

\[COS\phi = 0.128 \]

\[S = 7.7 KVA \]

\[Q= 7.63 KVAr \]
